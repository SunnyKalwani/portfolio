Projects:

Restaurant Web App:

The Restaurant Web App ("ULU's Kitchen") is a comprehensive digital platform designed to enhane the dining experience for both customers and restaurant owners. It seamlessly combines stunning visuals, intuitive navigation, and Advanced functionality to provide users with an immersive and convenient way to explore the restaurant's offerings.
This app allows customers to easily browse menu, place orders online, and make reservations. Additionaly, the restaurant web app empowers owners providing them with tools to manage orders, update menu items, track inventory, and analyze customer feedback, ultimately streamlining operations and fostering business growth.
